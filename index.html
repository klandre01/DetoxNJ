What's At Stake?


YOU are in danger of being poisoned within your own community:

Superfund sites are chemical dumps full of toxic waste lying right in the open -- and often right in the middle of communities. These sites have been created mostly by chemical manufacturers who actively fight against and avoid costs for site cleanup.


Even with New Jersey having the densest population in the country, we are also home to the greatest number of Superfund sites. 50% of NJ residents live within a 3-mile radius of a chemical waste dump, meaning 50% of NJ residents are vulnerable to a multitude of health risks.
